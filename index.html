Bedtime Roleplay

Activity 6 — Listen and Talk

📌 Language Focus
will + not = won't
📖

👆 Tap will or won't to fill in each blank!

👩 Mum
Remember to brush your teeth.
🧒 Child
I ___ .
👩 Mum
Don't forget to wash your face.
🧒 Child
I ___ .
👩 Mum
Don't stay up too late.
🧒 Child
I ___ .
🧒 Child
Mum, will you come and turn out the light?
👩 Mum
Yes, I ___ .
Fill in all 4 blanks to check!

🗣️ Now have a new conversation!

Use the words in the box. Tap one, then practise with a partner!

💬 Try this conversation:
👩 Mum: Don't forget to .
🧒 Child: OK, I will! / I won't!
